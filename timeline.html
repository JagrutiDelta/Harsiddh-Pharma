Timeline of Harsiddh Pharma Development

  1. 1987

    A small manufacturing facility was established at Rakhiyal Ahmedabad by Late Shri Popatbhai M. Mistry with a vision to provide pharmaceutical companies with state-of-the-art machinery.

  2. 1988

    India’s first Slant Single Head Ampoule Filling & Sealing Machine was developed.

  3. 1990

    A complete indigenous design of Linear Vial Washing Machine was developed.

  4. 1991

    India’s first Vertical Single Head Ampoule Filling and Sealing Machine was developed.

  5. 2001

    The company shifted to a bigger state-of-the-art ultra-modern manufacturing facility of 40,000 sq feet at VATVA Ahmedabad.

  6. 2005

    Rotary Vial Washing Machine was developed.

  7. 2007

    Entrusted with the manufacturing capacity and quality of HARSIDDH format part division for imported lines.

  8. 2008

    Vertical and Horizontal Cartonator Machine was developed.

  9. 2013

    Rotary Ampoule Washing Machine was developed.

  10. 2016

    Hi-Speed Ampoule & Vial Line was developed.

  11. 2017

    Nasal Line and Ophthalmic Filling Line were developed.

  12. 2018

    Servo Based Rotary Ampoule & Vial Washing Machine was developed.

  13. 2020

    Veterinary Plastic PFS Machine was developed.

  14. 2021

    MDI Line was developed.
